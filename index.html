Pittsburgh Coffee Rankings

Finding the best brew in the 'Burgh—one espresso at a time.

After traveling the world and using various sites to find the best coffee shops, I wondered: why are we stuck with mediocre listicles for Pittsburgh? I'm rating local coffee shops by flavor, price, variety, seating, and hours.

Redstart Roasters 20/25 cups, 16/20

  • Flavor: Smooth with a chocolate finish, subtly bitter yet refreshing.
  • Price: Espresso $3, Latte $4
  • Variety: Basics + specials like the Mole Latte.
  • Seating: Library vibes, plenty of chargers and comfy seating.
  • Hours: 7am–6pm daily
